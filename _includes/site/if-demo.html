Try it: retrain both models live

This demo runs a small isolation forest implementation in your browser with the same scoring formula as the library: 256-point subsamples and the standard path-length normalization. Both panels train on the same points. Brighter regions receive higher anomaly scores.

Click or drag on either panel to add points · shift-click to erase · both models retrain on every change. Look for the rectangular bands and cross-shaped artifacts on the left that the hyperplane splits on the right avoid.
