Try it: the kinematics behind the energy scale

Drag the two scatter vertices of a double-scatter event. The beam direction and the line between the vertices set the scattering angle θ of the first interaction, and exact two-body elastic kinematics convert θ into the recoil energy — no simulation, no signal model. Small angles reach below 1 keV; a full backscatter pins the 74 keV endpoint.

Drag scatter 1 along the beam line and scatter 2 anywhere in the volume · the marker on the curve follows your event · the energy scale comes from geometry alone, which is the point of the calibration.
