Try it: fly the transfer yourself

A normalized 2-D two-body simulator with tangential impulse control — the same setup as the workshop notebook. Start on the inner orbit (r₁ = 1) and circularize at the target (r₂ = 1.8) using as little Δv as possible. The analytic Hohmann transfer needs 0.249 — try to beat it, or press Run Hohmann to watch the textbook solution.

Hold a burn button (or the ↑/↓ keys) to keep firing · prograde is along your velocity · a clean transfer is two burns and a coast, not a stream of corrections.
